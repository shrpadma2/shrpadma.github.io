About Me

Shreyas Sunku Padmanabha

Ever since I started realizing that data is everywhere on the internet, the inquisitiveness guided by a fresh approach to worldly matters has always been my path. Be it the questions I encounter during a travel journey or those numerical problems in math or physics, I always have a way of finding the solution to them. Data Science, with its wide variety of data, is a treasury of problems to be solved. I strongly believe that my problem-solving and decision-making skills, powered by precise analytical thinking, could assist in serving the field of Data Science.

After completing my undergrad, I spent the next 1.5 years working at Cognizant developing an insurance application dealing with large amounts of data, which sparked my interest towards data science. Later, I interned at Rubixe as a data science intern, which gave me a solid foundation in data science.

I am currently pursuing my Masters in Data Science at The George Washington University, Washington DC, USA. I am also looking for summer intern opportunities in '23 as a Data Science intern.

Data Science Graduate Student with a love of helping others and problem-solving. My strong communication skills, along with my technical skills in Data Science and Machine Learning, Data Viz, SQL, Excel, and Tableau, allow me to transform data into impactful insights that lead to the best business decisions.

When not working or futzing around with data, you will find me gaming, listening to music, exploring new places, playing sports, or spending time with my friends or family.
